Our Story

A Legacy of Elegance & Warmth

Nestled in the heart of Machakos Town, New Ikuuni stands as a beacon of refined hospitality, offering an unforgettable blend of modern African warmth and timeless elegance.

Our spacious, thoughtfully designed interior creates the perfect ambiance for intimate dinners, family celebrations, and corporate gatherings alike. Every detail has been curated to ensure your experience with us transcends the ordinary.

From our award-winning local cuisine to our meticulously crafted cocktails, exceptional service is at the heart of everything we do. Welcome to your home away from home.
